PASSLOCK V1.0

A simple password manager using Java SWING and Netbeans IDE - keeps list of passwords in a text file, located in "Documents" folder (Windows)

DOWNLOAD at www.nunospencer.com

Report bugs, suggested improvements at www.nunospencer.com/#contact

***DISCLAIMER*** Passwords are kept in plain text - no encryption is used. The app is for demonstration purposes only, and although it works as intended, I am not responsible for privacy/security issues that may arise from use. USE AT YOUR OWN DISCRETION.
